Opportunities

If you reach out about a potential graduate or postdoctoral position, please include a CV and a brief description of your background and interests.

Graduate students
Students from a variety of backgrounds (e.g. biology, math, physics, computer science, engineering) are encouraged to apply. If you are already a graduate student at Harvard (or another area institution) and are interested in our work, contact Michael Desai to discuss opportunities. Otherwise, the lab is affiliated with several graduate programs at Harvard, so there are a number of possible routes to join the lab, including through the Department of Organismic and Evolutionary Biology (OEB), the Department of Physics, the Molecules Cells and Organisms (MCO) program, the Systems Biology program, as well as others such as Biophysics and SEAS. Potential applicants should contact Michael Desai in advance of submitting their application.

Postdocs
We are not actively looking for new postdocs at the moment. However, if you are potentially interested in a postdoc position, please email Michael Desai.

Undergraduate students
Positions for undergraduate students interested in experimental evolution or evolutionary theory are available. Interested students should contact Michael Desai.
